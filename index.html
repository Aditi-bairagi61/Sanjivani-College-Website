Civil Engineering

Civil engineering is one of the disciplines of engineering that involves designing the infrastructures like roads, buildings, pipelines, sewage systems, railways, and many more.

Computer Engineering

Computer Science Engineering is a course that deals with the design, implementation, and management of information systems of both software and hardware processes. A computer science engineer specialises in the theory of computation and the design of computational systems.

Electrical Engineering

Electrical Engineering course is that branch of Engineering that deals with the study of the application of electricity, electronics and electromagnetism. Trained electrical engineers to design electric circuits and equipment.

About Us

TSanjivani College of Engineering (An Autonomous Institute), Kopargaon is one among the premier technical institutes in Maharashtra state in the un-aided sector. Established in 1983 by the Sanjivani Rural Educational Society under the charismatic leadership of Shri. Shankarrao G.Kolhe, the Institute had its vision for social transformation and , upliftment of rural masses through education, training and research.
